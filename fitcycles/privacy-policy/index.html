Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the FitCycles app (hereby referred to as the "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Weijin Wang (hereby referred to as the "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed with your privacy as its absolute top priority. We believe that what you do in your workout app is your business alone. Therefore, the Application collects absolutely no personal data, no anonymous usage data, and no other information from its users. Your workouts, your timings, and your app usage remain entirely on your device.

Third Party Access

The Application does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect, use, or share your information. No data is transmitted to external services, and no third parties have access to any user information through this Application, because no user information is collected.

Opt-Out Rights

Since the Application collects no data, there is no information for you to opt-out of collection for. You can, of course, remove the Application from your device at any time by uninstalling it using the standard processes available on your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace.

Data Retention Policy (Not Applicable)

Since the Application does not collect any user data, there is no data to retain. All application functionality operates entirely on your device.

Children

The Service Provider does not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13, as the Application does not collect any data from any users.

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Since the Application collects no data at all, this point is moot for the Application itself, but we strongly encourage all children to never submit any personally identifiable information through any Application and/or Services. If you are a parent or guardian and you have concerns about your child's use of this app, please contact us.

Security

As the Application does not collect or store any user data, there is no user information that needs safeguarding. All app functionality and any data (like your custom timers) reside solely on your device.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes to the Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of 2025-07-25.

Your Consent

By using the Application, you are consenting to this Privacy Policy, which explicitly states that no data is collected by the app.
